About us

The main activity of MDCL is "Construction of water bodies". We carry out dredging to create and maintain the specified navigational dimensions both in the existing offshore access channels and in the water areas of the ports, as well as in the under construction - to ensure the safe and efficient operation of the transport fleet.

We are confident that maintaining depths through dredging is a strategic direction for the operation of any marine infrastructure facility. Any expansion and / or development of any port is not without dredging: whether it's building new berthing lines or expanding the water areas.

Procurement of dredging works is carried out by customers through the procedure of open tender. MDCL, commensurate with its capabilities, participates in open tenders for the purchase of dredging.

From the first day of its activity, MDCL seeks to promote transparent business, fair tenders for dredging contracts, careful pre-project soil surveys and compliance with high environmental standards.

MDCL strives to promote the prestige and reputation of the dredging profession. When implementing projects, all the planned works are organized in compliance with the requirements of the legislation and have the necessary regulatory and feasibility study. We adhere to relevant national and international standards.
